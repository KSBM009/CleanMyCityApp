Welcome to Clean My City

At Clean My City, we believe in the power of community action to create positive change. Our platform connects passionate individuals who are committed to making a difference in their communities through volunteer-based cleaning activities.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Are you ready to roll up your sleeves and help keep our cities clean and beautiful? Whether you're an individual looking to make an impact or a group eager to organize a cleanup event, Clean My City provides the tools and resources you need to get started.


Why Clean My City?

Our cities are facing a growing waste crisis, with plastic pollution and other environmental issues threatening the health of our communities and ecosystems. By joining Clean My City, you can be part of the solution and help create a cleaner, greener future for all.


Join Us in Making a Difference

Together, we can create cleaner, greener communities for future generations.
